The Electrical Engineering Department at VNIT offers a comprehensive M.Tech program, which spans two years and includes core courses, electives, and a research project in the final year under faculty supervision. The department specializes in Integrated Power System (IPS) and Power Electronics and Drives (PED), aiming to provide in-depth knowledge and practical experience in these fields.
